# British Airways Customer Review Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 3,000+ British Airways customer reviews to measure overall passenger satisfaction, identify key service drivers, and uncover areas of underperformance.

**Type:** Group Project    
**My Contribution:** Data analysis and visualization  
**Tools:** Microsoft Excel  

## Problem Statement
Customer feedback is scattered across ratings and comments, making it difficult to identify what truly drives satisfaction and where improvement is needed.

## Key Findings
- Overall BA ratings have declined consistently from 2014 to 2023
- In-flight entertainment and value for money are the weakest service categories
- Economy class passengers report the lowest satisfaction across all cabin types
- Ground staff service scores lower than cabin staff across all cabin classes
- BA scores 2.65/5 overall, significantly below competitors like All Nippon Airways (4.18) and EVA Air (3.93)

## Recommendations
- Reallocate ground staff resources toward economy routes
- Invest in in-flight entertainment and food quality
- Benchmark service standards against top-rated airlines
- Track satisfaction monthly to detect declines early

## Data Source
Kaggle — British Airways Reviews Dataset
